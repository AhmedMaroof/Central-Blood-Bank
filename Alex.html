The German Hospital is the first medical facility in Alexandria. On the wide range of medical services offered by the hospital, it has a strong commitment to providing specialized medical care to patients at a time when they need international quality standards and German expertise. The German Hospital is located in the heart of Alexandria in Saba Pasha area on a total area of 5000 square meters and height of six floors with a private entrance and two separate entrance to the emergency. The hospital is licensed by the Egyptian Ministry of Health to operate as a general hospital with all medical specialties No. 52 on March 19, 1993. 40 beds were added and 20 beds were added over the past ten years. The German heart center for catheterization, diagnosis and open heart surgery was also added to the treatment service abroad.
