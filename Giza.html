Zohairy Hospital exists to deliver the highest quality care to the most people possible at the lowest possible cost. It is a leading private multispeciality hospital located in the heart of Cairo. The hospital was established in 1985 by Dr. Abdelfattah Mohamed el Zohairy. Dr. Abdelfatah was a professor in anesthesiology at El Kasr Al Ainy, Cairo University. For more than 30 years the hospital has a consistent commitment to advancing its medical care, and providing high-caliber medical services to meet the needs, wants, and expectations of our patients. Today, we are a leading private hospital in the healthcare industry in Cairo, with an excellent reputation in outstanding health-care services through care, commitment, and perseverance.

Alsalam Hospital is a private independent medical hospital, founded in August 1982 by professor Dr. Fathi Iskander and a group of executive highly qualified doctors and successful businessmen dedicated to the principle of working together as a team to offer comprehensive and effective private medical services based on the latest standards of patient care. It is located in the heart of Cairo, in the famous district of Mohandessin, Alsalam hospital is considered one of the finest advanced care hospitals in Egypt and the Middle East. The latest and most sophisticated specialized equipment has been installed to meet a wide range of diagnostic, therapeutic, and other specialized services. Through nearly three decades of successful operation, the hospital has been able to maintain a high standard of patient care, due to the excellency of its leadership, its top physicians who are leaders in their respective fields, and its dedicated staff.
